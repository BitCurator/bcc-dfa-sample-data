What is an electronic interface?

An interface can best be described as a point of contact that uses a computer in the conduct of state business between Kansas State agencies and the Federal government. In each case, the point of contact must be ready and able to conduct business using their computers and software at the start of the year 2000 (Y2K). If an interface, because of Y2K computer problems on either the Federal or Kansas end, is unable to function at the start of the millennium, serious potential disruptions could exist. This holds true not only with respect to the economic impact to the state, but also in the manner of how we care for citizens.
