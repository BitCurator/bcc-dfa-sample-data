Mission Critical 1 Custom Applications Repair Status

Shows total amount of time estimated, incurred, and remaining to repair Mission Critical 1 Custom Applications. These are computer systems tailored to perform unique processes or functions.  Failure of a Mission Critical 1 asset will result in wide public safety, health, welfare, or economic impact, or it will have serious liability consequences for the state and there is no viable "work around."

Shows the percentage progress in terms of the total number of Mission Critical 2 Custom Applications to be repaired.  Mission Critical 2 Custom Applications are assets that will result in a serious inability to perform normal government operations if a failure occurs, but a "work around" is achievable for a limited period of time.

 

 Shows the percentage progress in terms of the total number of Mission Critical 3 Custom Applications to be repaired.  Mission Critical 3 Custom Applications are assets that will have limited impact on the public or government operations, or a viable "work around" is achievable for a sustained period of time.

 

Mission Critical 1 Hardware   Status
(1,966 Assets)

Shows the percentage progress in terms of total number of Mission Critical 1 Computer Hardware Assets.  These assets are defined as personal computers (PC's), minicomputers, mainframes, etc. that are essential to critical government services.  Failure of a Mission Critical 1 asset will result inwide public safety, health, welfare, or economic impact, or it will have serious liability consequences for the state and there is no viable "work around."
