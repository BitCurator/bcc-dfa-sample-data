Year 2000 Remediation Contract
Local units are invited to take advantage of this state contract for Year 2000 problem remediation services

As information on local units of Kansas government becomes available, it will posted here. We will have information on the repair efforts of cities, counties, school and water districts.

We are actively encouraging local units of government to submit information on their efforts at Year 2000 remediation. This information may be faxed together with official letterhead to the State of Kansas Year 2000 Awareness Center at (785) 296-1168. This service is being provided at no cost for local units of government.

To facilitate submissions, the following format is suggested:

1 - Name of Town, City or County
2 - Address
3 - Owner name/Point(s) of Contact
4 - Telephone number(s)
5 - E-mail Address

6 - Mission Critical Computers

Please state whether an assessment has been done about potential problems and percentages completed. Whether repair (or replacement) has been completed or percentage completed and if the equipment has been tested for Year 2000 compliance.

7 - Mission Critical Software

Please include the same types of information about your software, e.g. assessment, repair (or replacement) and testing percentages.

Please also feel free to tell us about any other mission critical or emergency items such as communications equipment, telephone systems, water distribution and its readiness status.
