Personal Computer (PC)

Warning! Before attempting to test your PC for Year 2000 compliance, make sure you backup your files!

To check out your PC's BIOS (Basic Input/Output System), here are some starting points…

  • AMI 2000 Corporation's bios checking software
  • Test 2000 and Year 2000 from RighTime Co
  • National Software Testing Laboratory's Ymark2000
  • The US Army Infantry Center at Fort Benning, GA has a useful Y2k site. Their Year 2000 Compliant PC Internal Clock and BIOS page and Test Your PC System Clock page offer some testing models you can do yourself.
  • Intel's support services department's Prepare your PC contains information on testing your PC for Y2K compatibility.

Y2K Resources for Local Government and Business

The Y2K problem doesn't just affect citizens; it will also touch local government functions and the ability of businesses to continue to engage in commerce without disruption.

Local Government

  • The Texas Guidebook 2000 offers probably the most comprehensive treatment to date on managing Y2K issues at the local government level.
  • Minnesota Year 2000 Official State of Minnesota site with best practices models, contingency planning guides and much more.
  • Y2K Preparedness: a Community Action Plan - Be sure to follow the link to "Human response to disaster".
  • Public Technology, Inc.'s Y2K Sampler

  • American Water Works Association Y2K Problems and Solutions
  • Y2K Contingency Planning for Local Government By Victor Porlier
    •
  • The Five Questions Every City and County Should Ask
  • National Association of Counties Y2K page
  • The Cassandra Project info on numerous local issues
  • State and Local Government Y2K Success Stories from itaa.org
  • Facing Your Year 2000 Liability - legal issues for local governments

Small Business

  • Small Business Administration. The Small Business Administration has one of the most comprehensive sites for addressing Year 2000 business issues.
  • National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)
    Provides free software tools to help small business prioritize assets, develop contingency plans, and conduct remediation activities.  It also maintains a Year 2000 Help Center for small business issues.
  • The Small Business Advisor The Small Business Advisor has helpful suggestions and "things to do."
  • Dr. Ed Yardeni's Economics Network Center for Cybereconomics The Chief Economist of Deutsche Bank Securities in New York explores Y2K issues here.
  • Year 2000 Network Effects Discusses issues relevant to your business supply chain.
